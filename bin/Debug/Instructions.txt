HOW TO USE THIS DEMONSTRATIVE:

     ----> Load an image, at menu Files-->Load Image.
			----> load an isometric 2d image, others images type dont will work..
     ----> Select the rotate plane (plane XY, XZ, or YZ), into combobox with tag: "Choice Plane Rotate".
     ----> click in "Create Rotate Images" to view the demonstrative of image rotate, of object 3d.
     ----> click in "Create Rotate Animation" to view the demostrative of animation with objects 3d rotates.
     ----> alternative: write in "rotate angle" to modify the angle of rotate images,
		 then click "Create Rotate Images" to wiew images rotates.
